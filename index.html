https://www.britannica.com/technology/television-technology Nov 10, 2024

A television is an electrical device and media that broadcasts information, sound, and moving images to a viewership. From a mechanical creation in the early 20th century, it has developed into a crucial component of contemporary news, education, advertising, and entertainment. TV functions by transforming audio and video into signals that are sent via cable, the internet, or broadcast over the air.
